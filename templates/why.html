What is Unwanted Spam?

Unwanted spam is when someone sends you a message, like a text or an email, to advertise something without you asking for it.

Why is it important to comply with the law?

Complying with the law helps to protect your rights and privacy. If someone sends you unwanted spam, you can file a complaint and seek compensation for any damages caused by the receipt of unwanted messages.

Examples of Unwanted Spam

  • Receiving a text message from a store advertising a sale without you signing up for their messages.
  • Getting an unsolicited email from a company trying to sell you a product.
  • Getting a message on your phone from someone you don't know trying to sell you something.
